## Purpose of Research
You are being invited to participate in an engineering research study that aims to develop and test a wearable sensor system for analyzing movement patterns. This research is conducted for educational and scientific purposes as part of an ISEF (International Science and Engineering Fair) project.

**What this research involves:**
- Testing a wearable device with non-invasive sensors
- Recording finger movement, muscle activity, and motion data
- Contributing to the development of machine learning algorithms
- Helping improve assistive technology engineering

**What this research does NOT involve:**
- Medical diagnosis or treatment
- Clinical assessment of health conditions
- Invasive procedures or medical interventions
- Long-term health monitoring

## Procedures
If you agree to participate, here is what will happen:

### Data Collection Session
1. **Duration**: Each session will last approximately 10 minutes
2. **Frequency**: You may participate in 1-3 sessions
3. **Location**: [Specify location - school lab, etc.]
4. **Equipment**: You will wear comfortable sensors on your hand and arm

### What You Will Do
- Wear non-invasive sensors (flex sensors, EMG sensor, motion sensor)
- Perform simple hand movements and exercises
- Follow basic instructions from the research team
- Provide feedback about comfort and usability

### Data Collection
- **Sensor readings**: Finger position, muscle activity, hand motion
- **Session information**: Date, time, duration of testing
- **Technical data**: Device performance metrics
- **No personal information**: No names, addresses, or identifying details stored with sensor data

---

## Privacy Protection and Data Security

### Anonymization
- Your identity will be protected through anonymization
- You will be assigned a random session code (e.g., "SES_A7K9M2X1")
- No connection between your name and the sensor data
- All data files use only anonymous session identifiers

### Data Security
- All data is encrypted using advanced security methods
- Stored on secure, password-protected systems
- Access limited to authorized research team members only
- Regular security reviews and updates

### Data Usage
- Data used solely for engineering research and education
- Results may be presented at science fairs and academic conferences
- No individual participants will be identifiable in any presentations
- Data may be used for algorithm development and testing

---

## Risks and Benefits

### Potential Risks
**Minimal Risk Level**: This research involves minimal risk to participants
- **Physical**: Slight skin irritation from sensor adhesive (very rare)
- **Privacy**: Risk of data breach (minimized through encryption and anonymization)
- **Time**: 10-30 minutes of your time per session
- **Discomfort**: Minor inconvenience from wearing sensors

### Risk Mitigation
- Non-invasive sensors only
- Immediate removal if any discomfort
- Strong data protection measures
- Right to stop participation at any time

### Potential Benefits
- Contributing to assistive technology development
- Learning about engineering research methods
- Supporting innovation in wearable technology
- No direct personal benefits guaranteed

## Voluntary Participation and Withdrawal

### Your Rights
- **Voluntary**: Participation is completely voluntary
- **No penalty**: No consequences for declining to participate
- **Withdrawal**: You can stop participating at any time
- **Questions**: You can ask questions before, during, or after participation
- **Data deletion**: You can request deletion of your data

### How to Withdraw
- Simply inform the research team of your decision
- No explanation required
- Data collected up to that point can be deleted upon request
- Withdrawal will not affect any relationships with the school or research team

## Data Retention and Deletion

### How Long Data is Kept
- **Raw sensor data**: Maximum 2 years
- **Processed anonymous data**: Up to 5 years for research continuity
- **Research results**: May be kept indefinitely (fully anonymized)

### Data Deletion
- Secure deletion procedures follow industry standards
- Complete removal from all systems and backups
- Deletion certificate provided upon request
- Regular purging of expired data
